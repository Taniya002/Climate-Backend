# References — Water Management Skill

## Irrigation method efficiency
| Method | Efficiency | Water savings vs flood |
|---|---|---|
| Flood irrigation | 40% | 0% (baseline) |
| Sprinkler irrigation | 70% | 20% |
| Drip irrigation | 90% | 35% |

## Drought risk band to recommendation tier
| Drought risk | Tier | Action |
|---|---|---|
| 0-29 | Low | No change needed |
| 30-60 | Moderate | Scheduling optimization (timing, mulching) |
| 61-100 | High | Recommend drip irrigation upgrade if not already in use |

## Notes on estimates
Water savings percentages are based on published agronomy ranges for
irrigation method efficiency, not site-measured values. They are always
presented as estimates, never as guarantees, and the Evaluation Engine
treats them as directional confidence rather than verified accuracy.
